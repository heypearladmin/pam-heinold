# Image briefs — Pam Heinold authority site

Pam prefers **real photography or licensed stock — no AI-generated imagery**. These briefs are written so they work for:

- Stock photo searches (Unsplash, Pexels, Adobe Stock, Shutterstock, Getty)
- Briefs to Pam's eventual photoshoot
- Briefs to a video editor cutting reels and tours

The visual style is **cozy • elegant • relatable** per the brand kit — warm professionalism, lifestyle-focused, never cold or flashy. Pensacola context wherever possible.

Save each delivered file to `public/images/` using the **exact filename** listed. The TSX is already wired to those paths.

## 2 · Hero images

### `hero-pensacola.jpg` *(Homepage hero — 16:9, 2400×1350 minimum)*
**Stock search:** "Pensacola East Hill historic neighborhood golden hour", "Pensacola Florida historic homes oak trees", "Florida Panhandle historic neighborhood".
**Brief:** A wide, cinematic Pensacola street scene at golden hour. Mature live oaks arching over a quiet street. Historic Florida architecture (East Hill or downtown). Warm amber light. Cozy, inviting, Southern lifestyle feel. Light fog or haze acceptable. No cars in foreground if possible.

### `relocation-hero.jpg` *(Relocation hero — 16:9)*
**Stock search:** "Pensacola Bay sunset", "Pensacola downtown historic skyline", "Florida Gulf Coast bay golden hour".
**Brief:** Pensacola Bay or Pensacola downtown at golden hour. Historic palms, water reflection, warm color palette. Calm and welcoming, not flashy.

### `home-relocation-feature.jpg` *(Homepage relocation accent — 4:5)*

**Brief:** A warm, well-photographed Pensacola or Gulf Coast waterfront home at sunset. Calm, residential, refined — single-family scale, no high-rises.

## 3 · Neighborhood images *(4:5 portrait)*

Each anchors the neighborhood card on the homepage and the hero of the dedicated detail page.

### `neighborhood-marcus-pointe.jpg`
**Stock search:** "Pensacola gated community golf course home", "Florida golf community luxury home", "Marcus Pointe Pensacola".
**Brief:** A traditional Florida home in an established gated golf community. Mature landscaping, brick or stucco facade, manicured lawn, hint of golf-course frontage in the background. Quiet, refined, old-money calm.

### `neighborhood-nature-trail.jpg`
**Stock search:** "Pensacola gated community contemporary home", "Nature Trail Pensacola", "modern Florida community home with amenities".
**Brief:** A more contemporary Florida home in a newer gated community. Modern stucco architecture, walking trails, glimpses of clubhouse-style amenities. Family-friendly, amenity-rich, well-tended.

### `neighborhood-east-hill.jpg`
**Stock search:** "Pensacola East Hill historic home porch", "Florida bungalow oak trees", "Florida Panhandle historic Craftsman".
**Brief:** A restored historic Florida home with a deep front porch, mature oak shading, classic East Hill architectural details. Warm afternoon light, lived-in, character-rich.

### `neighborhood-downtown-pensacola.jpg`
**Stock search:** "Palafox Street Pensacola", "Pensacola downtown historic district", "Pensacola historic Spanish architecture".
**Brief:** Historic downtown Pensacola — Palafox Street, Plaza Ferdinand, or one of the restored historic blocks. Spanish/colonial architecture, palm trees, warm storefront light, walkable.

### `neighborhood-pensacola-beach.jpg`
**Stock search:** "Pensacola Beach white sand turquoise water", "Pensacola Beach home sunset", "Gulf Coast beach community".
**Brief:** Pensacola Beach — white sand, turquoise Gulf, soft beach home in mid-distance. Year-round, social, warm. Aerial okay.

### `neighborhood-perdido-key.jpg`
**Stock search:** "Perdido Key Florida beach sunset", "Perdido Key luxury beach home", "secluded Gulf Coast beach".
**Brief:** Quieter, more secluded Gulf beach. White sand, calmer water, fewer people. Luxury beach home or beachfront condo in soft mid-distance. Calm, refined.
